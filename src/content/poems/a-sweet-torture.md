Early frisking hours of dawn,
The relentless creases of not-so-linen sheets,
Happy Hours, we say with a frown,
Irony, in all it's glory, ado, hearts beat,
Premature love-like longings, sweats of passion,
How vivid the sketch is, how beautifully broken in-time,
A new trend, a hippie fashion,
Ethereal musings frozen in mind!
